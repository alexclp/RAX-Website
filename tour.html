The main screen - your car's statistics center. It'll blow your mind.

The main screen offers you information about all the app's modules. It has different sections such as your total distance traveled, your average speed, and basic user information. It also shows a timeline of your last two trips which includes the starting and ending point, the total distance and average speed. The dashboard continues with to dos and days remaining for reminders, the parking position and time spent parking in that spot (if you click the widget), and lastly, the error code detector shows an error code, if your car has any.

Oh yeah, it's that good. See for yourself.

The iConnect device detects the way you drive and based on that it can give you a skill and a score that you can improve in time. This awesome feature helps you to save fuel and stop driving aggressively!

Your trip history just at your fingertips. See where you have been.

All your car info in just one place. Never forget anything.

With RAX, you can store info such as your car make and model, your car's VIN code and chassis code, the fabrication year of your car, and lastly your mechanic number which we'll be used to call him or her when you have a problem. Also, some of this data will be transmitted to your mechanic to identify your car much easily.

Your car documents and reminders stored here. Remember when to rotate your tires or renew your insurance.
