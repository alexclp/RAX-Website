What happens if I change my car?

If you change your car and it is manufactured after 2006, you can continue to use your app and the iConnect device with your new car!

How does the device comunicate with my iPhone?

The iConnect device plugs in your OBD2 plug that every car has (it is usually found under your steering wheel). After that, the device creates a Wi-Fi network to which your iPhone connects, and you're all set. If you don't have the device, you simply press the No Connection button and you have to select the "No device mode".

What iPhones are supported?

Any iPhone that is running iOS 6 or above.

Do I need an Internet communication to use the app?

No, because the app only uses your iPhone's GPS chip to get your location and speed which are written in a local database.
